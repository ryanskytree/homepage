## Overview

WatchLog is a multimodal framework that transforms ultra-long endpoint logs into video-structured behavioral representations. By converting events into key-value guided images and arranging them temporally, WatchLog enables video-language reasoning for endpoint security analysis.

## Key Contributions

*   Converts raw EDR logs into visual behavioral representations.
*   Models endpoint activities as video sequences.
*   Introduces temporal cross-attention for efficient long-context reasoning.
*   Produces interpretable behavioral explanations.
*   Significantly reduces computational costs.
*   Supports practical deployment in enterprise environments.

## Experimental Results

*   Built the EDR8M-20R benchmark dataset.
*   Outperformed state-of-the-art EDR baselines.
*   Achieved higher detection accuracy and recall.
*   Generated reliable reasoning traces for analysts.
*   Demonstrated strong generalization ability.

## Research Area

*   AI Security
*   Endpoint Detection and Response
*   Multimodal LLMs
*   Explainable Threat Detection

## Keywords

Cybersecurity, EDR, Multimodal LLM, Event Reasoning, Explainable AI

## 概述

WatchLog 是一个多模态框架，可将超长的端点日志转换为视频结构的行为了解。通过将事件转换为键值引导图像并按时间顺序排列，WatchLog 支持对端点安全分析进行视频-语言推理。

## 主要贡献

*   将原始 EDR 日志转换为可视化行为表示。
*   将端点活动建模为视频序列。
*   引入时间交叉注意力机制以实现高效的长上下文推理。
*   生成可解释的行为解释。
*   显著降低计算成本。
*   支持在企业环境中实际部署。

## 实验结果

*   构建了 EDR8M-20R 基准数据集。
*   超越了最先进的 EDR 基线。
*   实现了更高的检测精度和召回率。
*   为分析师生成了可靠的推理轨迹。
*   展示了强大的泛化能力。

## 研究领域

*   AI安全
*   端点检测与响应
*   多模态LLM
*   事件推理
*   可解释AI
