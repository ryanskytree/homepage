## Biography

I am an Algorithm Engineer at 360 Company, with a Master's degree from Universiti Sains Malaysia. I was a visiting student at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the guidance of <a href="https://minyang.me/">Prof. Yang Min</a> and <a href="https://www.xinhai.co/">Prof. Tan Minghuan</a>, and an intern at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) under <a href="https://yangmenglinsite.github.io/">Prof. Yang Menglin</a>. My publications include ICASSP and ICML, and my main research areas are Large Language Models, Graph Neural Networks, Incremental Learning, and Multimodal Learning.
