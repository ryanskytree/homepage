## Biography 

I am a researcher working at the intersection of Graph Machine Learning, Continual Learning, AI Security, and Large Language Models (LLMs). My research focuses on developing efficient, scalable, and interpretable learning systems for graph-structured data and cybersecurity applications. 

My recent work spans replay-free continual graph learning, graph diffusion mechanisms, multimodal reasoning systems, and endpoint security intelligence. I am particularly interested in designing practical AI systems that can continuously learn from evolving environments while maintaining efficiency, privacy, and explainability.

## 个人简介 

我是一名研究员，专注于图机器学习、持续学习、人工智能安全和大型语言模型（LLMs）的交叉领域。我的研究重点是为图结构数据和网络安全应用开发高效、可扩展且可解释的学习系统。 

我最近的工作包括无重放持续图学习、图扩散机制、多模态推理系统和端点安全智能。我特别感兴趣于设计能够从不断变化的环境中持续学习，同时保持效率、隐私和可解释性的实用人工智能系统。
